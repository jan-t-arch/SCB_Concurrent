此原型的已知未定項Open questions in this prototype

以下項目尚未有 AC 或未經確認,目前的做法僅屬假設。No AC covers these yet. What the prototype does today is an assumption, not a decision.

  1. 全部選取時,AC11 問題應完全隱藏,還是顯示但非必填?此處採用「隱藏」。When all policies are selected, is the AC11 question hidden or shown as non-mandatory? This prototype hides it.
  2. 部分選取時 AC11 與 AC7.2 兩項均為必填;需確認兩者為獨立控件,而非合併為一項。On partial selection both AC11 and AC7.2 are mandatory. Confirm they are two separate controls, not one merged question.
  3. 情況 4(選 B 但只有本案)假設無須填寫任何內容,直接通過。Situation 4 (option B with only this case) is assumed to pass with nothing to fill.
  4. 在選 A 時所作的決定,於其後選 B 的申請會否保留?Do decisions made under option A carry over to the later application that picks option B?
  5. AC7.2 選項二:中文「有待刪除的未完成個案」與英文 Redundant case 意思不一致。AC7.2 option 2: the TC wording (incomplete case pending deletion) does not match the EN "Redundant case".
  6. AC17 只在產生 PDF 一刻檢查;本原型只涵蓋 SAVE 這一關,未包含產生 PDF 那一關。AC17 runs at Gen-PDF. This prototype only covers the SAVE gate, not the Gen-PDF gate.
  7. AC19 目前只在選項 B 顯示;AC 並未說明是否只限某一選項。若選項 A 亦須顯示,計算應否包括唯讀表內的保單?AC19 currently shows only under option B. The AC does not say it is limited to one option. If it must also show under option A, should the total include the policies in the read-only table?
